HERE IS ABOUT OUR ORGANISATION

.
JAI GROUP SECURITIES AND ALLIED SERVICES, a leading provider of professional manned security services, fire & safety, and facility management solutions in Tamil Nadu, India. We are a new company that has started operations in 2019 with a mission to deliver top-notch security and facility management services to our clients.​
.
Our company is committed to providing comprehensive security services to ensure the safety of your property, assets, and people. We have a team of highly trained and experienced security personnel who are skilled in various security protocols, techniques, and technologies. Our security personnel are equipped with the latest gadgets and equipment to ensure that your property is secure round the clock
.
We also specialize in providing fire and safety solutions to protect your property from potential fire hazards. Our team of fire and safety experts is trained in identifying potential fire hazards, implementing preventive measures, and providing emergency response services. We use state-of-the-art fire suppression systems, detectors, and alarms to ensure that your property is protected from any fire-related incidents.
.
In addition to our security and fire & safety services, we also offer comprehensive facility management solutions to help you manage your facilities efficiently. Our facility management services include housekeeping, pest control, waste management, landscaping, and other related services. We take care of all your facility management needs, so you can focus on your core business activities.
.
In our company, we believe in providing customized solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients. We understand that every client has different security, fire & safety, and facility management needs, and we work closely with our clients to develop customized solutions that meet their specific requirements.
.
We are committed to delivering quality services, and we achieve this by investing in the latest technologies, training our staff regularly, and maintaining a strict quality control process. Our company is licensed and certified by the relevant authorities, and we comply with all the local laws and regulations.
.
We are confident that our services will meet and exceed your expectations. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you protect your property, assets, and people.
